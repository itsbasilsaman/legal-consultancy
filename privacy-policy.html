Privacy Policy

Khibrah Admin App

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Khibrah Admin ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, shares, and protects information when administrators use the Khibrah Admin application. The platform is operated in accordance with the laws and regulations of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. By using the Khibrah Admin app, you ("admin" or "you") agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

As part of providing full administrative functionality, we collect the following types of information:

1. Administrator Information:

  • Full name, phone number, email address
  • Login credentials (phone, Google, or Apple authentication)

2. Platform Data Accessed by Admins:

  • Registered client and lawyer profiles
  • Wallet balances and payout requests
  • Session details and chat metadata
  • Uploaded video insights for review
  • Subscription plan details and settings
  • Banner content and promotions

3. Usage and Log Data:

  • Actions performed in the admin panel
  • Timestamps and device/browser information
  • Error logs and diagnostics

How We Use the Information

The information collected is used to:

  • Enable admins to manage the Khibrah and Khibrah Lawyers apps
  • Facilitate verification and processing of lawyer payouts
  • Review and moderate uploaded video content
  • Monitor session activity and handle disputes
  • Create and manage subscription plans
  • Enforce platform policies
  • Improve platform security and performance

Data Security

We implement strong administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect sensitive data. This includes:

  • Role-based access control for admin users
  • Encrypted storage of sensitive information
  • Secure transmission protocols (HTTPS)
  • Regular audits of admin actions

Your Rights

As an admin user, you have the right to:

  • Access your own account activity
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Report misuse or suspicious activity
  • Request clarification on monitoring

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your responsibilities as an admin, please contact us at support@khibrah.sa
