Privacy Policy

Legally Admin App

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Legally Admin ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, shares, and protects information when administrators use the Legally Admin application. The platform is operated in accordance with the laws and regulations of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. By using the Legally Admin app, you ("admin" or "you") agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

As part of providing full administrative functionality, we collect the following types of information:

1. Administrator Information:

  • Full name, phone number, email address
  • Login credentials (phone, Google, or Apple authentication)

2. Platform Data Accessed by Admins:

  • Registered client and lawyer profiles
  • Wallet balances and payout requests
  • Session details and chat metadata (but not private message content)
  • Uploaded video insights for review
  • Subscription plan details and settings
  • Banner content and promotions added via the admin dashboard

3. Usage and Log Data:

  • Actions performed in the admin panel
  • Timestamps and device/browser information for admin activity
  • Error logs and diagnostics

How We Use the Information

The information collected is used to:

  • Enable admins to manage the Legally and Legally Lawyers apps
  • Facilitate verification and processing of lawyer payouts
  • Review and moderate uploaded video content
  • Monitor session activity and handle client-lawyer disputes
  • Create and manage subscription plans, promotions, and banners
  • Enforce platform policies and handle violations
  • Improve platform security, performance, and reliability

Data Security

We implement strong administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect sensitive data. This includes:

  • Role-based access control for admin users
  • Encrypted storage of sensitive information
  • Secure transmission protocols (HTTPS)
  • Regular audits of admin actions to prevent misuse

Your Rights

As an admin user, you have the right to:

  • Access your own account activity
  • Request correction of inaccurate profile information
  • Report misuse or suspicious activity to the platform owner
  • Request clarification on how your administrative actions are logged or monitored

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your responsibilities as an admin, please contact us at support@legally.sa
