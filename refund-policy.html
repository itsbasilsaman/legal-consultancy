Refund Policy

Khibrah - Legal Consultancy Services

Introduction

This Refund Policy outlines the terms and conditions for refunds of services provided by Khibrah. We are committed to ensuring customer satisfaction while maintaining fair and transparent refund practices in accordance with Saudi Arabian consumer protection laws.

Eligibility for Refunds

Refundable Services

  • Initial consultation fees (if no legal advice has been provided)
  • Subscription fees for unused periods
  • Document preparation fees (if work has not commenced)

Partially Refundable Services

  • Legal representation fees (pro-rated based on work completed)
  • Document review services (based on completion percentage)
  • Training sessions (if cancelled with sufficient notice)

Non-Refundable Services

  • Completed legal consultations
  • Filing fees paid to government agencies
  • Completed document preparation
  • Services already rendered or completed

Refund Process

To request a refund, please follow these steps:

  1. Submit a written refund request to our support team
  2. Include your account details and reason for refund
  3. Provide any relevant documentation
  4. Wait for our review and response (typically within 5 business days)

Processing Time

Refund Processing

  • Approved refunds will be processed within 10-15 business days
  • Refunds will be issued through the original payment method
  • Processing times may vary based on your payment provider

Special Circumstances

We understand that exceptional circumstances may arise. In such cases, we will review refund requests on a case-by-case basis, considering:

  • Medical emergencies
  • Force majeure events
  • Technical issues preventing service delivery
  • Service quality concerns
